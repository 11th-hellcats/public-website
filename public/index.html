About

We’re an Australian Hell Let Loose clan centred on clean comms, defined roles, and respectful games. New and experienced players welcome. Regular Oceania scrims and community nights.

How to join

  1. Hop into Discord and say g’day in #recruitment.
  2. Tell us your preferred role: SL, Support, Armour, Recon, or Infantry.
  3. Join an open night for a quick familiarisation.
  4. Add the clan tag once you’re settled.
