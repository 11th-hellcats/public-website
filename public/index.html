11th Hellcats

A competitive, console-only, Hell Let Loose clan.

About

We are a competitive, console-only, Oceania Hell Let Loose clan. We currently compete in The Premier League (TPL), Off-Season Competitive League (OSCL), and Winter War League (WWL), along with regular scrims against other Oceania clans.

The 11th is always open to new members, no matter what your experience level. We offer regular games, a relaxed vibe, regular training and skills improvement, and reward initiative.

How to join

The 11th is always open to new members, no matter what your experience level. We offer regular games, a relaxed vibe, regular training and skills improvement, and reward initiative.

  • Hop into Discord and say g'day, or go to #join-the-team.
  • Find any member with an 11TH tag in-game, on any server, and say hi

Minimum expectations

  • Have a mic, be willing to talk, and be ready to work in a team
  • Be available during a reasonable number of game times (usually Sunday day and Wednesday evening in Australia and New Zealand)
  • Attend training when you are available
  • Check the 11th Discord weekly for game rosters and updates
